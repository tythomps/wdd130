Rafting Site Plan

Tycen Thompson

Overview

Our Purpose is to help you get down the river safely, teach our customers how to paddle, give you a lesson on the river and the surrounding area, and handle all the details of your trip to our rafting company

Our Target Audience is anyone who wants to learn the ins and outs of Water Rafting, people who are looking for a fun time with their family doing somehting that they have not done before and looking for a new thrill. Q: How old do you have to be to participate? A: 6 years and up Q: How many people can you take per raft? A: We can take between 5-6 per raft, and a 7th spot is for our instructor for your raft Q: How much does it cost? A: it is $7 per person on our rafts and there is also a launch fee of $3 for the group
